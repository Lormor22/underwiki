Focus Death

Classes with this skill: Wind Rider, Ghost Hunter.

For 2 minutes, decreases user's Critical Rate by 30% and frontal Critical Damage by 30%. Increases the probability of deadly attack by 60% and rear Critical Damage by 90%. Requires a dagger.

Armor Crush

Classes with this skill: Titan, Fortune Seeker, Maestro.

Supplements the user's P. Atk. with 3077 Power to stun the enemy for 8 seconds and decrease their P. Def. by 30% and M. Def. by 30%. Requires a sword, blunt weapon, two-handed sword or two-handed blunt weapon. Over-hit and critical hit are possible. Ignores Shield Defense.

Warrior Servitor

Classes with this skill: Arcana Lord.

For 5 minutes, causes the servitor to be possessed with the spirit of an ancient warrior. Increases Max HP by 20%, HP Recovery Bonus by 20%, P. Def. by 20%, Accuracy by 4, P. Atk. by 10%, Atk. Spd. by 20%, and Resistance to de-buff attacks by 10. Decreases Speed by 10%. Consumes 10 Spirit Ore.

Dimension Spiral

Classes with this skill: Warlock, Arcana Lord, Elemental Summoner, Elemental Master, Phantom Summoner, Spectral Master.

Creates a tear in the dimension to inflict damage on the enemy with 98 Power added to M. Atk. If a monster is targeted, inflicts additional damage with 109 Power. Over-hit is possible.

Servitor Empowerment

Classes with this skill: Warlock, Elemental Summoner, Phantom Summoner.

For 30 seconds, increases the servitor's P. Def. by 3600, M. Def. by 2700, and Resistance to buff-canceling attacks by 80%. It is unable to move while the skill is in effect.

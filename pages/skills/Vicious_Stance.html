Vicious Stance

Classes with this skill: Warrior, Gladiator, Warlord, Rogue, Treasure Hunter, Hawkeye, Scout, Plains Walker, Silver Ranger, Assassin, Abyss Walker, Phantom Ranger, Orc Raider, Destroyer.

Increases Critical Damages by 35. Continuously consumes MP proportionately to the user's level.

Tip: The increased critical damage from this skill only affects autoattacks and blow-type skills.

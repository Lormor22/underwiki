Touch of Death

Classes with this skill: Hell Knight, Shilien Templar.

Sacrifices oneself to pronounce an Dark curse on the enemy. Cancels up to 5 buffs with 25% probability and consumes 90% CP. For 2 minutes, decreases Max CP by 90% and the power of received HP recovery magic by 30%. Increases susceptibility to de-buff by 30%.

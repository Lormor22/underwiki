Force Mastery

Classes with this skill: Monk, Tyrant, Grand Khavatari.

Absorbs the target's energy. Energy can be absorbed with a 15% chance for regular physical attacks and 30% during a critical attack. Applies when a hand-to-hand combat weapon is equipped. Up to 8 stage of energy can be absorbed.

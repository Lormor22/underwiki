Blinding Blow

Classes with this skill: Plains Walker, Abyss Walker.

Attacks the target's vital points with 3653 Power added to P. Atk. and turns his/her back to you. Increases Speed by 40. Requires a dagger. Over-hit and Half-kill are possible.

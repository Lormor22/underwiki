Dark Vortex

Classes with this skill: Soultaker, Storm Screamer.

Stirs up a vortex connecting to the dimension of darkness and inflicts dark damage on the enemy with 139 Power added to M. Atk. Absorbs 25% as HP. For 30 seconds, decreases Accuracy by 6 and Resistance to Dark by 30. Consumes 12 MP every second.

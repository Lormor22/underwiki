Arcane Chaos

Classes with this skill: Archmage, MysticMuse, Storm Screamer.

For 30 seconds, decreases Resistance to the enemy's buff-canceling attacks by 30%, Resistance to de-buff attacks by 30% and drains 24 MP every second. Increases physical skill MP consumption by 10% and magic skill MP consumption by 30%.

Parry Stance

Classes with this skill: Dreadnought, Titan, Fortune Seeker, Maestro.

Blocks an attack using a weapon. Increases P. Def. by 25% and M. Def. by 25%. Decreases Speed by 10%, Atk. Spd. by 20%, and Accuracy by 4. Continuously consumes MP proportionately to the user's level.

Spirit Sharing

Classes with this skill: Warlock, Elemental Summoner, Phantom Summoner.

For 20 minutes, increases the user's P. Atk. by 5%, M. Atk. by 5%, skill P. Atk. by 10%, Atk. Spd. by 7%, Casting Spd. by 5%, and Critical Rate by 10%. The master's abilities are extended to the servitor.

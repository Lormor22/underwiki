Judgment

Classes with this skill: Dark Avenger, Shilien Knight.

Inflicts dark damage on the enemy with 2104 Power added to P. Atk. and greatly provokes the enemy. For 30 seconds, decreases Critical Damage by 35% and Resistance to every type of close range weapon by 20. Requires a sword, blunt weapon, or dualsword. Over-hit and critical hit are possible.
